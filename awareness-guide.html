Strategy 1

üí¨ Personal Messaging

Most Effective

Why It Works

  • Creates personal obligation and commitment
  • 10-20x higher response rates than public shares
  • Builds deeper understanding and engagement
  • People respond to personal requests, not general broadcasts

Implementation Framework

Phase 1: Your Inner Circle (Day 1)

Send to 10-15 closest friends and family members:

  • Start with: "I need your help with something important"
  • Share the personal impact
  • Make a specific request
  • Express why their support matters to you

Phase 2: Extended Network (Days 2-3)

Send to 20-30 acquaintances and colleagues:

  • Adapt message to relationship level
  • Keep it concise but heartfelt
  • Focus on the injustice/fairness angle
  • Make the ask easy to say yes to

Phase 3: Strategic Contacts (Days 3-5)

Reach out to people with specific influence or connections:

  • Community leaders
  • People in relevant industries
  • Those with large social networks
  • Media contacts if available
