The Situation We're Addressing

A family in Italy (#FamigliaNelBosco - "Family in the Woods") has had their 3 children separated from them following a dispute with state authorities.

The children have been moved to a facility where the mother can visit but cannot stay with them. This family's children were removed not for abuse or neglect, but for choosing an alternative lifestyle: homeschooling, living close to nature, and limiting screen time.

They have been separated for almost a month, missing Christmas and New Year together as a family.
